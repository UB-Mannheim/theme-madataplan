Welcome to the Data Management Plan platform hosted by the Mannheim University Library

The Data Management Plan (DMP) platform supports researchers in the process of creating a Data Management Plan. As a member of the University of Mannheim, you can log in using your university ID.

DMPs can help to think through the key aspects of research data management, such as legal and ethical considerations, IT security, documentation and quality, as well as archiving and sharing.

Many research funding bodies require you to submit a DMP or at least to address research data management in the proposal (see DMP requirements on our website). Therefore, this platform provides several DMP templates that guide you step-by-step through funder-specific checklists, e.g. DFG, ERC Grant, EU Horizon, to meet their requirements.
